# Background

The McKinnon Center for Management is the staple of UNMâ€™s northern edge of campus.  The multi-story building was constructed as a
replacement for the deteriorating Anderson School of Management building, and the $5 million funds were donated to UNM by Ian and Sonnet

2016.  The building adjacent to MCM previously known as the Graduate School of Management has now assumed the Anderson School of Management
